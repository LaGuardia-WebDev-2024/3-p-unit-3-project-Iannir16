Macarons

  • Time: 2 hrs 10 mins
  • Serves: 16
Ingredients Quantity
Egg whites 3
White Sugar 1/4 cup
Confectuiners' Sugar 1 2/3 cups
Finely Ground Almonds 1 cup

Step 1: Line baking sheet with a silicone baking mat.
Step 2: Mix Ingredient, the egg whites and white sugar untill they have a soft fluffy texture.
Step 3: mix ingredient, sift the confectuoners' sugar and ground almonds in a separate bowl.
Step 4: mix a bit of each bowls together to create the batter and then with a spoon pour the batter onto the sheet in small circle shapes.
Step 5: let the cookies stand out at room temp untill they form a hard skin on top for about an hour.
Step 6: preheat the oven to 285 degrees F then set the cookies in the oven for about 10 mins.
Step 7: add the original batter on one cookie before placing another one on top.
Step 8: Enjoy.
